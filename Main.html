Entitlement of Leave

All the employees are entitled for 24 leaves in the accounting year and hence 2 leaves will be credited to their account on first day of the month. Accounting year for the purpose of leave will be from January to December. In case if an employee joins in the mid of the year then he will be entitled for leave on the prorata basis for the remaining period of the accounting year.

Normally an employee can avail maximum leaves up to available leave balance credit up to that month. In case of continues leaves, intervening weekly offs/ holidays will be treated as leaves. All leaves has to be intimated, applied and sanctioned in advance before going on leave.

Leave without Pay

If an employee is taking more leaves than accumulated leave balance to his credit due to pressing requirement to go on leave to meet some unavoidable commitments; can be granted leave without pay (LWP) at the sole discretion of management. This is however granted only in exceptional cases. The implications of leave without pay are as follows.

No salary and no monetary benefits whatsoever during the period of leave.

An employee taking leave without prior sanction of leave will be treated on LWP. However in these cases reasons for absence and the circumstances under which the employee could not get his/her leave pre-sanctioned are first investigated and then the leave is regularized as paid leave at the sole discretion of management.

Compensatory Leave

All employees who are asked to work on weekly-off days or overstay on working days are entitled for compensatory leave. Compensatory leave will be credited as ¼ day for every completed two hours of work. No compensatory leave will be credited for instances of less than 2 hours. Such Compensatory leave will be merged with the leave entitlement. All the compensatory leave shall be entered in the Leave Card immediately on the next day of extra work and submitted for approval.

Short Leave

All the employees are expected to report to the duty on or before their stipulated reporting time in general but not later than 10 minutes after stipulated reporting time in exceptional circumstances.

Management recognizes that due to issues related to vehicle or traffic rush an employee might get delayed beyond 10 minutes. Management will grant such delays in reporting to the duty beyond 10 minutes but not later than 30 minutes from stipulated reporting time as SHORT LEAVE. Such instances of grant of Short leaves are two instances per calendar month; third instance and onwards will treated as each of 1/4 leave.

Management also recognizes need for leave to attend any personal work during working hours. Management will grant one instance per month of 1 hour leave per calendar month during working hours.

Short leaves shall not be clubbed with each other or merged with any other leave.

Short leaves shall not be treated as right to claim and hence Short Leaves will not be carried forward. All instances of Short leaves shall be recorded in the leave card and sanctioned by sanctioning authority.

Leave Accumulation & Encashment

Accounting of leave is done based on calendar year from January to December. Leave can be accumulated and Leave Balance including Compensatory leave will be carry forwarded to next leave accounting year. At the end of Leave accounting year, employee whose accumulated leave balances exceeds 15 days can opt of encashment of leaves exceeding 15 days.

The encashment is done at the rate of salary paid for the month of December of that year. Payment for encashed leave however shall be done by 31st January of the following year. If an employee do not opt for encashment, all accumulated balances will be carry forwarded to next Leave accounting year.

Leave encashment request will not be entertained during mid of the year. Leave encashment request shall be forwarded as per attached format.

Sanctioning Authority

Leave Cards for each employee will be maintained separately and will be kept with administration department at all the times. Employees can collect their leave card from administration department for leave application and return back to administration after taking sanction of leave from Management. Leave will be sanctioned as per the balance and in line with the exigencies of the work.

All employees are requested to co-operate and follow the company leave rules.

AutoCAD Guidelines

While preparing CAD drawings, please keep following things in mind.

  1. One should never use lines, only p-lines to be used.
  2. Every hatch must be associative and with its Boundary.
  3. No separate leaders to be provided, Use Standard Leaders from MSA Standard.
  4. In model space, only relevant and final information shall be kept. All the unnecessary information must be deleted.
  5. Create blocks as and when necessary. Name of blocks must be relevant. Also make sure the blocks are not in a layer that is unplottable. Such as Gen_Viewport Boundary. The blocks shall be placed in relevant layer and not in layer 0.
  6. Use of array is forbidden.
  7. No layer other than MSA Standard Layers should exist in the drawing. If due to some reason you are not able achieve this, please speak with the team leader.
  8. Frozen or Turned Off layers should be deleted before releasing the drawing.
  9. Size of Text, Dimensions, and Annotations must be as per the scale of Viewport in Layout and MSA Standards.
  10. Model Space must contain only the drawing objects. Legends, Key Plan, Tables etc must be in the layout (paper) Space only.
  11. Level guides must be kept back in draw order.
  12. Grid lines must be kept in front in draw order.
  13. Key Plans should not contain any unnecessary Text/Data. Relevent Objects to be Highlighted.
  14. The left most bottom corner of your building shall be placed at (0,0,0) cordinated in model space. This shall not be changed afterwards.
  15. Everything Should be aligned in Model Space and Layout Space, Follow the links provided below for reference:
    • Alignment - Model Space
    • Alignment - Layout Space

Design Basis Approach

Contrary to what one is led to believe in college/ institute, architecture is not simply an art. Creativity is always appreciated; however, a lot of money, time, and resources are at stake in a typical project. Therefore, you are supposed to justify your ideas. Design basis approach will help you achieve that.

At MSA, at whatever level you are engaged in, you are expected to present your ideas with some design basis. Examples of design basis would be official specification document, official client requirements, local bylaws, national standards, Handbooks, reference guides, scientific papers, etc.

Work or ideas without a solid design basis will almost always invite revisions. Therefore, you are requested to understand this concept and adopt it in your daily project delivery.

Email Etiquette

General Email Guidelines

  1. Subject of email should be relevant to the items being sent via email. The subject of email must have the name of the project_Site / plot name if applicable_Type of Drawings / Documents being sent. The name of the project must form the beginning of your email subject line. Make sure this name of the project is maintained all the communication related to the project throughout the course of the project cycle. Following are a few examples;
    • GSRTC Bus Terminal_Navsari_Site Layout
    • GSRTC Bus Terminal_Navsari_Sections & elevations
    • GSRTC Bus Terminal_Navsari_Tender Documents
    • GSRTC Bus Terminal_Navsari_Electrical Drawings
    • CCEL Head Office_UGWT
  2. If you are writing a follow-up email, make sure you send the email in response to your earlier email. The follow up email must always have a trail email.
  3. If you are issuing drawings / documents to clients, consultants, or contractors, make sure the link to outbox folder is embedded in the email.
  4. Readable PDF copies should always accompany the drawings / documents you decide to release via email.
  5. File titles of all the documents / drawings shall be strictly as per DCI.
  6. Attach a PDF copy of Transmittal mentioning all the documents / drawings being sent through the email
  7. Any drawing being released must never have Autodesk Educational stamp. This also applies to drawings from other consultants that you release.

Internal Communication via Emails

  1. Team members shall send their work to their leaders via email. 'For Checking' shall be used in the beginning of email subject body.
  2. Leaders shall send their comments to their team members via email. 'Comments' shall be used in the beginning of email subject body.
  3. You all are also requested to use email to share your work with Interior, estimating, and 3D visualization teams. While sending you files, 'For Working' shall be used in the beginning of email subject body.
  4. You are requested to self checke drawings/ documents as against MSA Standards.
  5. Please also make sure you document your own comments remarks on the drawing / document using AutoDesk Design Review.
  6. Revise the drawing / document based on your own assessment.
  7. Send the revised drawing / document to the team leader for checking along with Commented copy (your own comments).
  8. The reviewer has the right to reject the document / drawing without explanation if the document / drawing is not found in alignment with the MSA Standards

Asana Guidelines & workflow

  1. Everyone's Asana tasklist must refelect their latest work assignments.
  2. Minimum one week's planning must be maintained in Asana. Everybody must update their tasklist for the next week on the Saturday before the upcoming week.
  3. Team leaders and project owners must monitor task due dates on a daily basis.
  4. All caps is not acceptable anywhere in Asana. Tasks and projects with either names or description written in all caps will be deleted without intimation.
  5. All the parties involved in the project must be added as followers for all the tasks of the project. Mamta Shah must be added as follower in all the tasks.
  6. If you are working on something, make sure you have been assigned a task in Asana for the same. If not, speak with your superior immediately.
  7. Once, the task is completed at your end, please assign the task back to the party who assigned you for their review.
  8. For project related tasks, task name and description must be as per DCI. The drawing / document code will become your task name and the drawing / document description will become your task description in Asana.
  9. For non-project related task, following are some task names examples;
    • Email - So and So Person
    • Followup - So and So person
    • Call - So and So Person
    • Meeting - So and So Party
    • Site Visit - Site Name
  10. For any challenge with Asana, speak with Devang immediately.
